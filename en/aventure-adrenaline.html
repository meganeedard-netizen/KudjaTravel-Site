Themed Trip

Adventure
& Adrenaline

The Caribbean, thrill-seeker edition

The Caribbean isn't just beautiful to look at. It's there to be lived, surfed, climbed, jumped into. The sea crashing against the shores of Guadeloupe and Martinique is waiting for those who dare to throw themselves in. The forests of Basse-Terre hide canyons you can only reach by abseil. And the hilltops offer panoramas that only those who lace up their boots or get on a bike ever get to see.

This trip is for those who want to feel the Caribbean differently. Not from a sun lounger (even if the end of the day ends up there), but in action, in motion, in full adrenaline. Beginner or seasoned, there's something here for every level and every appetite for adventure.

What I organise for you in an Adventure trip:

01 — Guadeloupe & Martinique

Water sports

Dive into the crystal-clear waters of the Caribbean and let the rush of water sports take over. Guadeloupe and Martinique have everything you need to learn or push your skills further, with certified instructors and spots that are genuinely spectacular. Surfing the waves off Capesterre or Le Carbet for those who love to ride. Kitesurfing and wing surfing on the lagoons of Grande-Terre where the wind is perfectly steady. Windsurfing for those who want to combine technique with speed. Every discipline has its reference spots, and I guide you to the best schools and the best locations based on your level and the season.

02 — Guadeloupe & Martinique

Aerial activities

For thrill-seekers, seeing the Caribbean from the air changes everything. A gyrocopter flies low over the coast, beaches and forest with a sense of absolute freedom and views you simply cannot get any other way. Paragliding from the heights of Martinique gives you a gliding flight facing the Caribbean Sea with the Pitons as a backdrop. And for the bravest, a tandem skydive over Guadeloupe with a freefall above coconut palms and turquoise lagoon is an experience you do once and talk about forever. These experiences show you the Caribbean from a completely new angle.

03 — Guadeloupe

Canyoning

Basse-Terre is cut through by canyons that stay off the radar because you have to abseil down to discover them. Descending a waterfall on a rope, jumping into a natural pool carved into volcanic rock, squeezing between walls the sun almost never reaches: canyoning in Guadeloupe offers total immersion in the island's wild nature. This activity must be done with a certified guide, and I select the best operators to guarantee safety and quality. Introductory sessions for beginners are available, alongside more technical sessions for experienced practitioners.

04 — Guadeloupe & Martinique

Mountain biking

Get on your bike and explore the wild terrain of Guadeloupe and Martinique. Mountain biking trails offer a deep dive into nature that few other activities can match: dense tropical forests, coastal dirt tracks, technical descents down the flanks of La Soufrière or Montagne Pelée. There are routes for every level, from gentle family discovery loops to challenging circuits for serious riders looking for a real test in the wild. This activity can be done independently with a trail map, or with a guide who knows the paths and local wildlife inside out.
